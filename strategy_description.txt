█ OVERVIEW
The Breakout Lines + TP/SL is a technical tool that automatically detects high and low pivots, draws dynamic sloped trend lines (resistance and support), and upon breakout generates clear breakout signals together with a complete trade setup – Stop Loss level and three Take Profit levels based on user-defined Risk-Reward ratios.

█ CONCEPTS
The indicator was created to combine classic pivot point analysis with dynamic trend lines and automatic risk management in a single tool. It therefore serves as a signaling indicator while also being very helpful in assessing potential future price reaction zones.

The indicator generates a signal only when the second pivot has already been detected, which means some signals appear with a delay equal to the time needed for pivot confirmation.

Pivot High and Pivot Low
Local price highs and lows detected with a specified number of bars on both sides. They act as natural turning points and the foundation for building resistance and support lines.

Dynamic Trend Lines
Each line is drawn between two consecutive pivots of the same type and extended forward while preserving the calculated slope. This approach reflects current market dynamics far better than classic horizontal levels.

Breakout
The moment of a decisive line breach:
• upward through the resistance line → BUY signal
• downward through the support line → SELL signal

This is interpreted as a change in local market structure and the potential beginning of an impulsive move.

TP/SL with Risk-Reward
Immediately after each breakout the indicator automatically calculates:
• entry level (close price of the breakout bar),
• Stop Loss (based on ATR multiplier or percentage from entry),
• three Take Profit levels according to the chosen RR ratios (e.g. 1:1, 1:2, 1:3).

█ FEATURES
1. Pivot Settings
* Pivot Left / Pivot Right – number of bars required to confirm a pivot. The indicator generates a signal only when the second pivot is already detected, so some signals appear with a delay equal to the pivot detection time.
* Max extend bars from detection – maximum forward extension of the line from the moment the pivot is detected.

2. Line Limits
* Show maximum last N HIGH lines (0 = all) – limits the number of displayed resistance lines.
* Show maximum last N LOW lines (0 = all) – limits the number of displayed support lines.

3. Colors and Transparency
* Separate colors for High (resistance) and Low (support) lines – these colors are automatically used for signals, TP/SL levels and fills.
* Transparency (0-100) – transparency level for all lines.

4. Visual Signals
* Show BUY/SELL Labels – text labels “BUY”/“SELL” at the breakout bar.
* Show Triangle Signals – small triangles on the chart (enabled by default).

5. TP/SL Settings
* SL = ATR × or percentage + ATR period and multiplier.
* Three independent Risk-Reward ratios (RR) for TP1, TP2 and TP3.
* Option to show or hide individual SL, TP1, TP2 and TP3 levels.

6. Display
* Show or hide SL, TP1–TP3 levels.
* Colored fills for risk zone (red) and reward zone (green).
* Automatic deletion of previous objects when a new signal appears.

█ APPLICATIONS
The indicator performs particularly well in the following scenarios:

1. Breakout from consolidation or structure
When price moves along a trend line and then decisively breaks it – this is often the start of a stronger impulsive move.

2. Trading in the direction of the trend
In an uptrend, breakouts of resistance lines (high lines) tend to be more effective. In a downtrend, breakouts of support lines perform better.

3. Confirmation of other strategies
A good filter or addition to price action, supply & demand, and classic chart patterns. Example: a resistance zone is broken, RSI crosses above 50, and at the same time the downward trend line is breached – this creates a strong entry signal, provided there is no other major resistance near the current price.

4. Precise position management
Right after the signal you immediately see:
• clearly defined risk (SL),
• realistic profit targets (TP1–TP3),
• visual representation of the potential reward-to-risk ratio (colored fills).

█ NOTES
* Too small a value for Pivot Left/Right generates noise, while too large a value delays signals – it is worth adjusting these parameters to your personal trading style and chosen timeframe.
* The indicator generates a signal only when the second pivot has already been detected, so some signals appear with a delay equal to the pivot detection time.
